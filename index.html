Understand Your Numbers

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate)

The number of calories your body burns at rest to maintain basic life functions like breathing and circulation.

TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure)

Your total calorie burn in a day, including exercise and daily movement. This is your "maintenance" number.

BMI (Body Mass Index)

A simple calculation using a person's height and weight. Note: It doesn't account for muscle mass!
