What does this mean? ⓘ

Sedentary: Desk job, <5k steps/day.

Light: 6–8k steps/day or light workouts.

Moderate: 8–12k steps + 3–5 workouts/week.

Very active: Physical job or intense training.

What is BMI? ⓘ
BMI compares height to weight. It cannot distinguish fat vs muscle, so athletic people may show higher BMI.
BMR:
What is BMR? ⓘ
BMR is the minimum calories your body needs at rest. Eating below this long term harms hormones and recovery.
TDEE:
What is TDEE? ⓘ
TDEE is your true maintenance calories including activity. Fat loss or gain depends on calories relative to this.
